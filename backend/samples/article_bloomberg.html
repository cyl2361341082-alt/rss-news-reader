AI chip supply tightens as cloud budgets expand

Cloud providers are still expanding capital budgets, but lead times for advanced accelerators remain stretched.

That imbalance has shifted spending decisions away from experimental pilots and toward large clusters with immediate revenue potential.

Enterprise buyers are also accepting higher utilisation targets, hoping better scheduling software can offset hardware scarcity.

Suppliers say demand has broadened beyond frontier labs, making it harder for smaller teams to secure capacity on predictable timelines.
